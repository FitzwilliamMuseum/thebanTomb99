Objects from TT99 in Cairo

In December 2002 a special exhibition opened in the Egyptian Museum Cairo, called 'Hidden Treasures'. Timed to coincide with a conference to mark the centenary of the museum, it features both objects from storerooms around Egypt (and the Museum) which have never been seen before, as well as objects recently discovered in excavations.

We suggested two objects from TT99 for this exhibition, and we are pleased to say that they were both brought up from Luxor to Cairo for the show.
