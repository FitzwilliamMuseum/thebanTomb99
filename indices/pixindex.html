Picture index

Visitor suggestions prompted the inclusion of an index of the pictures on the site. I have produced a few pages which can be accessed from the above picture menu bar. Choose a page under the appropriate heading--there are approximately 25 100 x 100 images per page. These images take the form of thumbnail views, which when clicked take you to the page where the full-size image is to be found. If you don't see the image immediately on that page, don't forget to scroll down. Occasionally you may encounter an image which is not yet on a page but is on the server.

A set of photos from earlier work in TT254 may be found here.

The basic layout for the thumbnail pages was produced by Graphicconverter.

It should be noted that the image index has not been updated for some considerable time...
